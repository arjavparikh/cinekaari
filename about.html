About Us

Hello, We are Cinekaari

Cinekaari Films is an initiative aimed at telling stories through audio-visual medium. A corporate story, a brand story or a documentary: all stories are worth telling and woven here to create an experience. Passionate storytellers get together to tell all such interesting tales in exciting narratives.

Cinekaari is founded by Kaushangi Modi, an alumni of the National Institute of Design having specialised in Film and Video Communication. It collaborates with creative personnel and technicians at different levels depending upon the need of the project. Cinekaari is dedicated to tell stories through films.

Our Work Process

1

Research

A detailed in-depth understanding about where, how, when, why helps us knowing the project and every aspect related to the project.

2

Planning

Planning is setting up a solid base for the execution to happen. All the paper work, all the minute details are sorted before the project goes to the execution stage.

3

Implementation

The actual happening part of the project. The shoot for films.

4

Post Production

The final finishing and minute refinements after the editing and sound design.
